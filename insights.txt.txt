E-commerce Data Analysis - Insights

1. Top Customers:
A small group of customers contributes the highest revenue. These customers are important for business growth.

2. Product Performance:
Electronics products generate the most sales compared to other categories.

3. Customer Behavior:
Customers who purchase multiple times (repeat customers) contribute more revenue than one-time buyers.

4. Payment Trends:
Cash on Delivery (COD) orders have a higher cancellation rate compared to online payments.

5. Sales Trend:
Sales show variation across months, indicating seasonal demand.

Conclusion:
The company should focus on retaining top customers, promoting high-performing products, and reducing COD-related cancellations.
